# Property Tax Relief

## Anchor benefit

### 2024 - 2025

You are correct to be confused about the timeline. The New Jersey ANCHOR program did not issue benefits for the 2022 and 2023 tax years.

The reason for this is a change in the state's property tax relief program structure. The ANCHOR program, which replaced the Homestead Benefit, had been operating on a lag, with benefits being paid for a tax year several years in the past. To align the ANCHOR program with the new Stay NJ program, the state skipped the 2022 and 2023 tax years and jumped directly to the 2024 tax year.

This has caused some confusion and frustration for residents, particularly those who were expecting a benefit for those years. While the state argues that it's a matter of semantics and that they are simply moving to a more current benefit year, some taxpayers will lose out on a year of benefits due to the change in eligibility dates.

"check the status of your New Jersey ANCHOR (Affordable New Jersey Communities for Homeowners and Renters) benefit payment, you can use the online inquiry tool provided by the NJ Division of Taxation or contact them directly. ￼
Here are the ways you can check:
1. Online Inquiry Tool:
Visit the official ANCHOR website: https://www.nj.gov/treasury/taxation/anchor/.
Navigate to the "Check Benefit Status" section.
You'll need to enter your Social Security number (or Individual Taxpayer Identification Number) and your zip code.
The tool will then display your application status, whether it's processing, approved, or if the state doesn't have a record of your application.
Once benefits are being issued, the tool will also show the date your payment was issued. ￼
2. ANCHOR Hotline:
Call the ANCHOR hotline at 1-888-238-1233 or (609) 826-4282.
Be prepared to provide your Social Security number or Individual Taxpayer Identification Number when prompted.
You may experience longer wait times during peak periods. ￼
Important Notes:
Confirmation Number: If you filed online or by phone, ensure you have your confirmation number, as it serves as proof of your application.
Processing Time: The Division of Taxation typically distributes ANCHOR benefits within 90 days of application, unless additional information is required.
Application Deadlines: For the 2024 ANCHOR benefit, new applications could be filed until October 31, 2025. The specific deadlines may vary for future years, so always check the ANCHOR website for current information.
Benefit Amount: The amount of your ANCHOR benefit depends on your income, filing status, and whether you are a homeowner or renter. For example, homeowners with income up to $150,000 could receive up to $1,750 (including an extra $250 for seniors). Renters in that same income bracket could receive up to $700. ￼
By using the online tool or contacting the hotline, you can track your ANCHOR benefit payment status and confirm if it has been issued. "
